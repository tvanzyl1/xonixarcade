# 05 — UI, UX, and Juice

## Menu requirements
Main menu should include:
- big game title
- short flavour subtitle
- button: Solo vs Computer
- button: Two Player Versus
- button: How to Play
- button: Sound On/Off

## HUD style
Use large, rounded, polished panels.
Keep everything readable on a dark background.

## In-game readability
The player should instantly distinguish:
- safe area
- unsafe area
- active trail
- enemy positions
- owned area by player 1 vs player 2

## Required feedback
- trail glow while drawing
- danger tint when at risk
- area-fill ripple when territory is claimed
- floating percentage / score text
- burst effects on enemy hits or death
- celebratory win overlay
- panel transitions and button hover feedback

## Sound suggestions
If lightweight audio is added:
- move tick / hum subtle loop
- capture chime
- death zap
- menu click
- victory stinger

## Personality
Use playful arcade wording where suitable, for example:
- “Claim the Grid!”
- “Line Closed!”
- “Big Grab!”
- “Too Slow!”
- “Zone Master!”

Keep it clean, light, and energetic.
