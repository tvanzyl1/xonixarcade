How To Play

Close the Line

Move on safe territory to stay protected, then cut into open space to draw a live neon trail.

Reconnect your trail to any safe ground to seal off a region. Empty regions become yours.

Solo mode: drifting enemies bounce inside the wild zone. If they touch your live trail, you lose a life.

Versus mode: Player 1 uses `WASD`, Player 2 uses arrow keys. Crossing another player's live trail punishes them.

Area Battle ends on the timer. Lives Battle ends when one player remains standing.
